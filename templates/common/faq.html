FAQ

1. What payment methods do you accept?

We accept only paying on cash.

2. How long does shipping take?

Shipping times vary depending on your location and the shipping method chosen at checkout. Typically, orders are processed within 1-2 business days, and domestic shipping takes 3-5 business days. International shipping times may vary.

3. Are your watches covered by a warranty?

Yes, all of our watches come with a manufacturer's warranty. The warranty period varies depending on the brand and model of the watch. Please refer to the product description for warranty details or contact our customer service team for assistance.

4. How do I care for my watch?

Proper care and maintenance are essential to prolonging the life of your watch. We recommend avoiding exposure to extreme temperatures, moisture, and chemicals. Regular cleaning with a soft, dry cloth can help maintain the appearance of your watch.
